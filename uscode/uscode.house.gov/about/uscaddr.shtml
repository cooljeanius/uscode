Availability of the U.S. Code


The official printed bound edition of the United States Code and the CD-ROM edition of the United States Code are available for purchase through the U.S. Government Printing Office (GPO) by phone, fax, or mail.

To order by telephone, call (202) 512-1800, Monday through Friday, 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m., eastern time.

If you are ordering the CD-ROM, be sure to tell the operator that the Order Processing Code is
7864
and give the operator the stock number (052-001-00511-2 for the CD-ROM with the laws in effect as of January 2, 2001) and the price ($45 to addresses within the United States or $63 to addresses outside the U.S.).
If you are ordering individual volumes of the printed version, just give the operator the stock numbers and prices.

To order by fax, print an order form for the CD-ROM edition or an order form for the printed edition and fax it (with payment) to (202) 512-2250.

To order by mail, print an order form for the CD-ROM edition or an order form for the printed edition and mail (with payment) it to:

Superintendent of Documents
U.S. Government Printing Office
P.O. Box 371954
Pittsburgh, PA 15250-7954

GPO accepts checks, VISA, MasterCard, and Discover.
